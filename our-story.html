F.A.M.E. was founded in 2016 by Rosy Ahmed, the CEO of Purple Vogue Events.

Hana Ahmed

Born and blessed with an extra chromosome, Hana is the inspiration behind F.A.M.E and a friendly supporter of all our special F.A.M.E stars. Her love for fashion and dance and passion for art has been the foundation of building the annual showcase for the past 6 years.

Zahra Ahmed

A former counsellor at Emirates International School, Zahra is the co-founder of F.A.M.E. and holds a decade of experience working with young people of determination in the UAE and UK. She is currently under training to become an educational psychologist to provide support to schools across London.

Rebecca Shamji

An entrepreneur, model and designer, Rebecca is our fabulous Event Director and has made F.A.M.E. a massive showcase since its inception. She brings magic to the stage with her eye for aesthetics and creativity, along with her passion for community building.

Anouska Mason

A graduate in theatre studies, Anouska is our Production Coordinator for the past 6 years and specializes in bringing spectacular visual displays to life with the support of our production partner, SOLIDE productions. She is also an empathetic and caring friend to all our special children at F.A.M.E.
